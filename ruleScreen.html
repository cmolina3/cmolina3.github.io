How To Play

To start the game simply click the Play button.

A Pokemon will appear with text above it asking you to which one it is.

Type your answer into the textbox below and then click submit.

If your answer was correct your score will increase by 100 points.

If your score was incorrect one of the X's below will turn red.

Get three Pokemon wrong and you lose.

Be careful spelling is important as well as spaces but capatilization does not matter.
